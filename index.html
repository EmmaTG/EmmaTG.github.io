A Little About Me

I started my studies in Mechanical Engineering at the University of the Witwatersrand in Johannesburg, South Africa. After completing this I began am master’s studies at the University of Cape Town (UCT). In 2020 I completed my PhD in Computation and Applied Mechanics at UCT. I then took up a postdoctoral position at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität (FAU) Erlangen-Nürnberg in 2021 where I work computational modelling and simulation of the brain. I have used and explored many languages (Java, Python, C++), frameworks (Angular, React) and databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB). I also have experience in AWS. Personally, I also enjoy hiking, cooking and running.
